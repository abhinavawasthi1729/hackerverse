About Web Build-A-Thon

Web Build-A-Thon is a unique tech building program launched by SmartBridge in association with GoDaddy Academy exclusively for Students & Faculty to provide Hands-On Experience on building Websites. The program is packed with e-learning, tech bootcamps, mentoring and 36 Hrs. of web build-a-thon powered by GoDaddy Academy & SmartInternz Platform. The top web developers will be receiving exciting prizes & recognitions from GoDaddy Academy

Frequently Asked Questions


1. Is there any entry fee?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Web Build-a-Thon is completely free..

2. What is the Eligibility Criteria to Participate?

Any Student and faculty from India & Interested in building awesome websites can enroll.

3. I dont have coding knowledge but i am interested to participate. Can I?

Yes, you can enroll for the Web Build-a-Thon.

4. Can I participate as a Team?

No. This program is for individual eligible students & faculties only, team participation is not acceptable.

5. How does the 3 Days training will be organised?

The training program will be organized online via Zoom every day between 6:00pm to 8:30pm. You will receive an email with Zoom meeting links once the registration is successful.

6. Will there be any guidance provided to complete the projects?

Yes. SmartBridge mentors will be guiding you to build the Website.

7. Can I get mentor support to clarify my technical queries?

Yes. You must join the slack / telegram channel to interact or clarify your queries with mentor.

8. Can I work on project before completing the 3 days training?

Yes. You can work on project in parallel but it is suggested to attend live training to gain enough knowledge to complete project

9. What if I don’t work on the project

This program includes both Training and Website development, it is mandatory to build & submit the Website to receive the participation certificate.

10. How Will I Connect With Mentors?

Please join our Telegram group using the link to connect with mentors
