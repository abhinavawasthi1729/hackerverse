Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)


1. Is there any entry fee?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. RPA Build-a-Thon is completely free.

2. What is the Eligibility Criteria to Participate?

Any faculty, who is interested to learn & explore Robotic Process Automation. Faculty of engineering, diploma, Sciences, Business schools, Secondary Schools, etc. can participate in this program. Basic coding skills will be advantageous.

3. I dont have coding knowledge but I am interested to participate. Can I?

Yes, you can enroll for the RPA Build-a-Thon.

4. Can I participate as a Team?

No. This program is for individual eligible faculties only, team participation is not acceptable.

5. How does the 3 Days training will be organised?

The training program will be organized online via Zoom every day between 6:00pm to 8:30pm IST (GMT+5:30). You will receive an email with Zoom meeting links once the registration is successful.

6. Can I enroll for the projects on Day-1 of the program?

No. Project enrollment will be active on last day of 3 Days training program. A detailed orientation session on project build-a-thon will be organized on last day of the training program.

7. What if I attend Bootcamp and don’t work on Project?

In case if you don’t work on Project you will be receiving only Bootcamp Participation Certificate.

8. Will there be any guidance provided to complete the projects?

Yes. SmartBridge mentors will be guiding you to build the projects.

9. Can I get mentor support to clarify my technical queries?

Yes. You must join the slack / telegram channel to interact or clarify your queries with mentor.

10. What is project build-a-thon?

The project build-a-thon is a project development activity, where faculty will be enrolling in a project listed. SmartInternz platform has a project workspace where the respective project should be developed and submitted for mentor review. A detailed demo of the platform will be provided during the 3 Days training program.
